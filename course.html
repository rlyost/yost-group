The Curriculum

Six Modules. One Framework. Built for Two.

Each module builds on the last — so by the end, you have a complete, working financial system you both own.

1

How to Talk About Money Without It Becoming a Fight

The foundation. Before systems, spreadsheets, or budgets — you need a shared language. This module covers how couples approach money differently, why that creates conflict, and how to build a communication framework that works for both of you.

2

Building a Zero-Based Budget Together

Every dollar gets a job before the month starts. This module walks you through building a budget as a couple — including how to handle individual spending, shared expenses, and what to do when you disagree.

3

Managing Joint and Individual Finances

Combined accounts or separate? This module covers the different approaches couples use, the trade-offs of each, and how to set up a structure that gives both partners visibility and accountability without eliminating autonomy.

4

Eliminating Debt as a Team

Debt doesn't just drain your account — it drains your relationship. This module covers proven payoff strategies, how to stay motivated together, and how to stop financing things that lose value.

5

Saving and Investing for Shared Goals

Emergency funds, retirement accounts, big purchases, and long-term wealth. This module aligns your savings strategy with your shared vision — so you're both working toward the same future.

6

Planning for Major Life Milestones Together

Kids, homes, career changes, and retirement. Money decisions get harder as life gets more complex. This module prepares you to make big decisions together — with clarity, not conflict.
